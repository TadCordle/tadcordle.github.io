Dreams Projects

A Little Perspective (video)

An echochrome-like perspective puzzle game. It is one of the most popular puzzle games in Dreams, was nominated for "Creation of the Year" at the second annual Impy Awards, and was "Mm pick of the week" upon its release.

Brocery Store (video)

Brocery Store 2 (video)

Pew (video)

A 2D sidescroller/shooter created in one week for the one-word Dreams community jam (rules: your creation may only use one word in its entirety). It was voted 2nd place in the jam and was chosen as "Mm pick of the week".
