A Little Perspective
Game by Tad Cordle
Music by Michael Reilly
Narration by Jonny Kaufmann and Del Rhys

Buy the game or download the demo on Steam.



A Little Perspective is a grid-based puzzle game with unique twists revolving around line-of-sight, object permanence, and spatial awareness.

Rotate the camera to change your perspective - create new pathways, sidestep obstructions, and exploit impossible physics to solve the game's many non-euclidean challenges.

Game features:

- 200+ puzzles built around the game's mind-bending properties
- An open hub that gives you freedom to choose how you progress
- A compelling narrative that unfolds as you progress deeper into the game, with twists and turns as you gain new insights
- Multiple control schemes - customizable keyboard and gamepad controls + fully playable with just a mouse
- Released on Windows, macOS, and Linux/SteamOS (Steam Deck verified!)







The game is a remake/upgrade of my most popular Dreams project under the same name, which took about 3 months to build/release. The remake was built in my free time using Raylib and FMOD and took about 2.5 years to complete.
